Who We Are

AVNEI YAQAR is a premier multi-service company committed to delivering excellence across diverse industries.

Founded with a vision to provide exceptional quality products and services, AVNEI YAQAR has grown from humble beginnings to become a trusted name in Zimbabwe's business landscape. Our name, meaning "Precious Stones" in Hebrew, reflects our commitment to being a valuable and enduring partner to our clients.

What began as a small grocery supplier has evolved into a diversified enterprise offering premium groceries, catering services, corporate gifts, IT solutions, and construction materials. This expansion was driven by our customers' needs and our passion for excellence in every endeavor.

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2005

Foundation

AVNEI YAQAR was established as a small grocery supplier in Harare, focusing on premium imported goods for the local market.

2009

First Expansion

Added catering services to our offerings after recognizing the need for high-quality event catering in the corporate sector.

2012

Bulawayo Branch

Opened our second location in Bulawayo to serve the growing demand in the southern region of Zimbabwe.

2015

Corporate Gifts Division

Launched our corporate gifts service to help businesses strengthen relationships with premium gift solutions.

2018

IT Solutions

Expanded into IT equipment and networking solutions to meet the technological needs of our corporate clients.

2021

Construction Materials

Added construction materials and fencing services, completing our transformation into a full-service business solutions provider.
